Barbie doll wears her iconic black and white swimsuit
For adult collectors

Barbie Byron Lars Coco (2007)

Collectible doll, released in 2007.
She wears a white padded vest, a lace skirt,
bright pink gloves, and a jeweled pin twice the size of her head.

City Smart Barbie (2003)

The 2003 City Smart Barbie is the fifth highest-rated Barbie doll of all time.
It is the model wearing a black pencil dress, holding a small bow on each shoulder.
She wears her blonde hair up in a sleek hairstyle with big bangs.

Barbie Astronaut (1965)

Barbie was destined to embody “independence and modernity”, that is why when the space race began in the 60s,
Barbie did not hesitate to put on the astronaut suit, because women also reached for the stars.
Wearing a special metallic suit and white helmet, this iconic doll was ready to explore the universe.

Totally Hair Barbie (1992)

With its extra-long hair, this model became a favorite among millennials.
As accessories I brought a comb, hair ties and even a tube of gel.
There was no one who could resist touching or combing her long hair and, in fact,
it is the best-selling version in Mattel's history with some 20 million replicas.

Barbie for president (1992)

In 1992, Barbie became an executive and that same year she ran for the presidency of the United States
for the first time. Since then she would run in every election for the White House,
thus acquiring an infinite repertoire of suits. and lecterns..

Barbie Mermaid (2010)

With a closet full of haute couture dresses made by the best designers, Versace, Armani, Moschino, Dior...
what Barbie was missing was a mermaid outfit and in 2010 her dreams came true.
If on dry land she broke stereotypes under the sea she reigned in a fantasy world.
With a shiny mermaid tail and her wavy body, many girls made her their best bathing companion.

Barbie Skier(2010)

Barbie, whose full name is Barbara Millicent Roberts, has not only encouraged different generations of girls
to achieve professional excellence, but she has also encouraged them to practice sports,
from horse riding to surfing to skiing and she even has an Olympic Barbie.
which was launched in 1996, on the occasion of the Atlanta Games.
